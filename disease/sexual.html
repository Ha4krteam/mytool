Sexually Transmitted Diseases (STDs)

Basic Information

Bangla Name: যৌনবাহিত রোগ

English Name: Sexually Transmitted Diseases

Short Form: STD

Test Reports

Reports usually include the name of the disease, test results (Positive/Negative), and relevant levels.

Testing Methods

  • Chlamydia Test: Conducted using urine or swab samples.
  • Gonorrhea Test: Performed with swab or urine samples.
  • HIV Test: Blood sample tested using ELISA or Rapid Test.

Common Causes

Unprotected sexual contact, multiple partners, and using infected blood or instruments can lead to STDs.

Possible Complications

STDs can lead to Pelvic Inflammatory Disease (PID), infertility, and increased risk of HIV.
